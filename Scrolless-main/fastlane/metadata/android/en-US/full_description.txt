Scrolless helps you reduce short-form video consumption.

Scrolless needs the Accessibility permission to detect supported content and apply blocking rules

Current features:

- Block All to immediately lock supported short-form feeds
- Daily Limit to cap how long you can spend on them each day
- Interval Timer to alternate watch time and breaks
- On-screen timer overlay to keep your current session visible
- Usage tracking so you can see where your time went

Supported apps currently include:

- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts
- TikTok
- Facebook Reels
- Facebook Lite
- Snapchat

Scrolless is fully open source and does not require an account.
